RC'25 GPA Selection Sequence (80 Credits Priority)

New GPA Calculation Rules (RC'25)

  • GPA is computed over a maximum of Eighty (80) credits.
  • Only courses completed at Level 4, 5, and 6 are considered. Level 3 courses are excluded.
  • Courses are selected according to the following priority groups:
    1. Compulsory courses at Level 5 and Level 6.
    2. Elective courses at Level 5 and Level 6.
    3. Compulsory courses at Level 4.
  • Within each group, courses are arranged in descending order of Grade Point Value (GPV).
  • If the 80-credit boundary falls within a course, a Part Credit of that course is taken.
  • Category W (Industrial Training) and other non-GPA categories are excluded.
